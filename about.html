About the Gate Pass Management System

This system is designed to digitize and streamline the process of requesting and approving gate passes for students and hostellers. It provides a centralized platform for students to submit requests and for faculty (teachers and wardens) to manage them efficiently.

How to Use This System

For Students:

  1. Navigate to the main page and click the "Student" button.
  2. If you are a new user, first go to the "Student Registration" page from the top navigation bar to get your unique Registration ID.
  3. Fill in the gate pass request form with your details, including your Registration ID, reason for leave, and contact information.
  4. Click "Submit Gate Pass". Your request will be sent to the designated teacher for approval and will appear on your personal dashboard.
  5. You will be redirected to the Student Dashboard where you can track the status (Pending, Approved, or Rejected) of all your requests.

For Hostellers:

  1. Select the "Hosteller" button on the main page.
  2. Complete the hosteller gate pass form with all required details.
  3. Submit the request. It will be sent to the warden for approval.
  4. You will be redirected to your dashboard to track your request status.

For Teachers & Wardens:

  1. Select your role ("Teacher" or "Warden") on the main page.
  2. Log in using your credentials. If you don't have an account, use the "Sign up" link available on the login form.
  3. Once logged in, you will be taken to your dashboard where you can view all pending requests.
  4. You can approve or reject requests directly from the dashboard. The student/hosteller's dashboard will update accordingly.
